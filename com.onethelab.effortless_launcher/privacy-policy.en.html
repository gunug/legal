Privacy Policy

App Name: Effortless Launcher
Effective Date: 2026-04-28
Last Updated: 2026-04-28

This Privacy Policy explains how Effortless Launcher (the "App") handles your personal information.

1. Summary at a Glance

  • The App does not perform any internet communication. (The Android INTERNET permission is not requested.)
  • No information is transmitted to any external server or third party.
  • All data is stored locally on your device only.
  • The App contains no advertising, no analytics tools (Firebase, Google Analytics, Crashlytics, etc.), and no tracking SDKs.
  • There is no sign-up or login. The App does not collect identity information such as email, name, contact, or date of birth.

2. Information We Process

The App stores and processes the following information solely on your device:

2.1 Required

ItemContentPurpose
List of installed apps App display name, package name, icon Core launcher functionality (search, launch, display)
App launch history Package name and timestamp of apps launched through this App (most recent 50 entries per app) Hot Zone (frequently used apps) ordering and "Recently Used" display

2.2 Optional (User may decline)

ItemContentPurpose
Android usage statistics App launch timestamps from the past 30 days (queried only once) Initial Hot Zone warm-up
Notification counts Number of unread notifications per package Badge display on app icons

Notification content (title, body, sender, etc.) is never read. Only the count is recorded.
Declining the optional items does not affect the App's core functionality.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

The App neither requests nor uses the following information:

Location data · Contacts · Call logs · SMS · Camera · Microphone · Phone state · External storage · Advertising ID (AAID) · Device ID · IMEI · MAC address · Email · Name · Date of birth · Notification body/title

4. Storage and Retention

4.1 Storage Location

All data is stored exclusively in the App's private sandbox area on your device. External clouds, external servers, and other apps cannot access this data.

4.2 Retention Periods

ItemRetention Period
App launch historyMost recent 50 entries per app (older entries are automatically deleted)
Notification countsHeld only in memory; cleared immediately when the App terminates
Deleted-app recordsAutomatically removed after 1 year
Settings (e.g., protected apps list)Until you change them or uninstall the App

5. Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

The App does not share or transfer the collected information to any third party.

6. Your Rights

You may immediately and completely delete all information stored by the App at any time using the following methods:

  • Android system settings → Apps → Effortless Launcher → Storage → "Clear data"
  • Uninstall the App

Optional permissions (usage statistics, notification access) can be revoked at any time:

  • Usage statistics: Settings → Apps → Special access → Usage access
  • Notification access: Settings → Apps → Special access → Notification access

7. Payment Information

The App does not currently implement in-app purchases. If a donation feature is added in the future, payment processing will be handled by the Google Play billing system. The App will not directly collect, store, or transmit payment instrument information such as credit card or bank account details.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no identity information is collected at all, the App can be used safely regardless of age.
